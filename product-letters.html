LETTERS

3D cut letters

Flat Cut Letter are traditionally the most cost effective way to promote a company name or brand. Now available with a very broad range of colours and materials such as Acrylic ,Aluminium composite , Aluminium and stainless steel .In a very competitive market we are able to use either our laser or router cutting facilities and theses signs can be produced to very high standards whilst meeting your budget.

3D Built up letters

Visually, these are a very competitive way of promoting a 3D presentation of any lettering signage .When variety of letter depth are used to give varying effects ,the texts can give a striking effect and give excellent contrast to any background they are fitted to .With us having one of the largest letter making production unit ,these signs can be produced working very quick lead times at very competitive prices.

Push through letters

Push thru signs are a special type of sign where the letter ,logo or shape is routed out of a solid surface and thick acrylic is routed to match.

With our precision routers we are able to add a shoulder to push thru shape which assures a fit that prevent light from bleeding thru .By adding coloured vinyl to the acrylic we can also achieve thousands of colours.
