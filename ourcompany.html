Company Profile

“Saint-Gobain completes 350 years of successful years in year 2015”

S olar Safety Film Trdg.L.L.C proudly represent SolarGard Saint-Gobain window film product range and functions as official stockiest and distributor in India. Our key operation is located at Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. Our official distribution network is extended to United Arab Emirates, Oman, Yemen and East Africa.

Solar Safety Film Trdg import, stock and distributes the SolarGard Saint-Gobain Automotive window film and Clear Shield paint protection films through official authorized dealer ship under the state wise distribution channel in India. Our automotive products are sold with factory backed warranty through the official dealership to secure your investment.

Our product range includes Bomb Blast resistant Safety and Security window Films, Architectural Heat and glare control Films and Frosted glass decoration window films. Solar Safety Film trdg has a professional skilled window film installation team for the architectural and safety film installation anywhere in the world. Solar Safety Film Trdg can execute any challenging projects in safety and sun control films on high rise building or skylights.

Solar Safety Film Trdg promotes our corporate concept “EARTH POSITIVE AND CARBON NEGATIVE” with energy saving and sun control solutions to reduce the carbon footprints. As part of go green concept, Most of the corporate companies are switching their existing facility management into the energy saving concept s according to the vision of Indian Oman Green Building Council . Solar Safety Film Trdg helps to transform the existing structure into an energy saving concept by installing solar energy control film to reject the heat radiation and save energy spent on cooling. Solar Gard products can reject the heat upto 82% and helps to cuts down the energy consumption by 30%. We provide services of safety and sun control solutions without compromising modern concept of work area with maximum natural light exposure under the safety and protection of Solar Gard Saint-Gobain window films.
